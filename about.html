AboutUz Investment Ltd

UZ Investments ltd is an indigenous Nigerian Company registered in 1999 for the manufacturing of furniture. We specialize in the provision of functional and precise furniture for homes, offices and institutions. Our furniture depicts an elegant expression of luxury, while being very considerate with prices. We are also a leading company in this sector.

We provide a very up to date solution in furnishing. Each of our furniture is a master piece of a painstaking effort. Our product range includes kitchens, wardrobes, doors, office furniture, house hold furniture, partitioning, Library and Laboratory furniture for institution.

Our 3rd and the most recent factory located in Bankole Town, Ibeju-Lekki, Lagos is equipped with the latest machineries in the industry. Our staff are very well behaved gentlemen and ladies who have been trained to adhere to precise details in order to satisfy even the most demanding clients. We make bold to state here that our attention to details always result in the creation of more aesthetic and comfortable living space.

Our projects includes provision of functional and comfortable furniture for offices such as Conference Tables, Executive Tables and Swivel Chairs etc, homes such as Kitchen's, Wardrobes, Bars, Tv Stand and Design, Building and Supply of Laboratory Furniture and Accessories. We value highly the issue of Safety, Comfort and Flexibility in the design of our furniture. We have evolved over the years keeping up with the fashion trends of the day
